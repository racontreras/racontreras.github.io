Central America Terrain Map

I was born and raised in Guatemala, Central America before moving to the USA. I was inspired to create this Central America terrain map to share with others the varied terrain shapes that can be found down there. Central America is a part of the Mesoamerican biodiversity hotspot, which extends from northern Guatemala through to central Panama. The region has several active volcanoes (the Central America Volcanic Arc). The map terrain geo-processing was done in ArcMap, the shaded relief in Adobe Photoshop, and the vector styles in Adobe Illustrator.
