School-based Pertussis Outbreaks in Los Angeles County

I was inspired to create an infographics piece that tells a short story about the rise of pertussis outbreaks, a vaccine preventable disease, in Los Angeles County-based schools. The main idea about this piece is to describe the outbreaks reports, why this is an important issue (pertussis is vaccine preventable) and how the County of Los Angeles Public Health Department is using GIS techniques to monitor public and private schools where reported outbreaks are occurring.
