City of Palmdale Alcohol Outlet Map Analysis

The map analysis depicted here was a presentation I prepared on behalf of my former employer, the Substance Abuse Prevention and Control program, County of Los Angeles Public Health Department, for a townhall meeting organized by the City of Palmdale council in March 2015. The analysis demonstrated that zip code 93550 had almost 50 % of all liquor stores in that city, thus resulting in increased crime incidents and more liquor store licence violations.
